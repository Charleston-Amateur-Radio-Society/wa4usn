South Carolina Amateur Radio Emergency Services (ARES):  
<https://ares-sc.org/>

SC Tactical Communications Plan:  
<https://ares-sc.org/tactical-communications-plan/>

The statewide designated ARES simplex frequencies are:

- **146.595 MHz** for VHF with **PL 156.7** on transmit
- **HF** ± **3.9935 / 3.9900 MHz LSB** or ± **7.232 MHz LSB**
